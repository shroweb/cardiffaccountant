Things people ask before getting in touch.

If yours isn't here, just drop us a message.

Do I need to be based in Cardiff?

No. We work with Cardiff and South Wales business owners, and most records can be shared remotely if that's easier.

Can you take over from my current accountant?

Yes. We can explain what's needed and handle the usual professional clearance process once you're ready to move.

What should I send first?

Start with the basics: what you need help with, your deadline if you know it, and any recent accounts, tax returns, bookkeeping records or HMRC letters.

Do you work with both companies and sole traders?

Yes. We support limited companies, sole traders, freelancers, small employers and business owners who want clearer numbers.

What if I'm behind with accounts or tax?

Get in touch as soon as you can. We'll look at what's outstanding and tackle the most urgent deadlines first.

Ready to get your accounts sorted?

Tell us what's on your plate — a VAT return due next month, a backlog of bookkeeping, or just wanting to understand what you owe. We'll tell you exactly what's needed.
